Assignment 7: Fusion Exercise 2 - Teapot

Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up. -- Pablo Picasso
Tutorial Stamp
Custom Teapot
Artist Statement

The inspiration for this teapot was actually a penguin. I've always thought penguins looked like tea kettles or teapots when, especially when they bend down to eat or feed their babies. I wanted to make the beak a different color but none of the textures on Fusion 360 allowed me to. However, it was fun to make.
