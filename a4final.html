Artist Statement

Inspired by Fluxus art, our project aims to create a space where the audience becomes the artist in the fashion of Fluxus scores. Fluxus scores were created to reimagine the boundaries and disciplines of art, creating meaning through individual and subjective means. With a combination of plywood and cardstock to create a modular tree, each audience member can create a new version of the same art piece with a personal meaning. By focusing on a tree, which is a form of growing nature, the piece creates a sense of parallel magnification of conceptual ideas into a physical entity. While the piece grows into a tree, the meaning behind the tree also grows.

During this group project, I was mainly the leader. I feel like I had the clearest idea of what we were doing.

  • made the first draft of our schema (the one with the blue background)
  • in charge of the designs and decisions of the group
